The Apothecary Ritual

Skincare is not simply a treatment; it is an intimate ritual of self-appreciation. Follow these professional protocols created by our lead dermatologists to double the absorption kinetics and lift facial fascia.

  1. Atmosphere: Cleanse your hands thoroughly. Warm your clinical space and press a warm steam microcloth over your skin for 30 seconds to dilate vascular networks.
  2. Application: Dispense the designated dosage onto your clean palms. Do not rub vigorously. Instead, pat, press and roll the formulation across cheeks, forehead, chin and throat.
  3. Fascial Drainage: Using your knuckles or a clean Aura Rose Quartz roller, sweep in light upward motions from jawline to earlobes, and nasal folds to temples, encouraging lymph drainage.
  4. Sealing: Conclude by applying an Aura ceramide balm or mineral sun protection to secure a micro-moisture lock.
