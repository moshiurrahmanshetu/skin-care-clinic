Common Inquiries

Skin Consultations

Do I need to clean my face before arriving for my consultation?

To guarantee highly accurate diagnostic skin analysis, we suggest arriving with clean skin free of heavy makeup, sunscreen, or rich face oils. If you are coming directly from work, we provide gentle pre-cleansing luxury wipes and soothing cleansers in our dressing suites.
Clinic Policies

Is valet parking available at the Beverly Hills clinic?

Yes. Complimentary underground valet parking is provided for all clients receiving premium energy treatments (lasers, Morpheus8, chemical peels). Our secure, monitored parking entrance is located directly at the rear of the Atelier Luxury Blvd building on Atelier Court.
Clinic Policies

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

We require a minimum of 24 hours' notice for standard skin consults and 48 hours' notice for deep medical/energy treatments. Cancellations made outside of this period may incur a 50% treatment fee to compensate for pre-calibrated medical settings and therapist booking configurations.
